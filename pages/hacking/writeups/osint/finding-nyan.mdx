# Finding Nyan

## Description

CTF: STACK the Flags 2022

Jaga is on a road trip to find Nyan. Do you know which road is this in the
meteverse?

Note that for this challenge, the road name is case sensitive, example -
`STF22{Jln_Binchang}`. The road name should be the exact address seen on Google
Maps

## Solution

_Pwned by [@skytect](https://github.com/skytect) and
[@fishjojo1](https://github.com/fishjojo1)_

The video shows a very blurry view out of a car window, with Jaga riding around
somewhere in Singapore.

If we focus on one of the frames, we see the logo of the company doing the
construction in the background.

![Frame with Construction Banner](/images/stacktheflags-2022/finding-nyan-1.png)

Let's try to identify the company. We can use Google to find a BCA directory for
licensed builders at
[www.bca.gov.sg/BCADirectory/Search/Result](https://www.bca.gov.sg/BCADirectory/Search/Result?page=-1&pCLSSelected=%2C83%7CA1&pGrading=A1&d=1).

![BCA Directory](/images/stacktheflags-2022/finding-nyan-2.png)

With some knowledge of Chinese, I can identify the first character "九" (jiǔ)
corresponding to the "CHIU" in the directory. After a quick Google Search, I'm
able to confirm that the company is called
`Chiu Teng Construction Co. Pte. Ltd.` thanks to the presence of the red
triangle in the logo.

![Google Images Search of "CHIU TENG CONSTRUCTION CO. PTE. LTD."](/images/stacktheflags-2022/finding-nyan-3.png)

Taking note that the construction site was along a long straight stretch of road
next to a largely undeveloped forested area, we set out to scour the internet
for tenders for building construction they have procured. After a while, we
finally found the location. The tender was found on
[www.sgpbusiness.com/company/Chiu-Teng-Construction-Co-Pte-Ltd/government-procurement/agency](https://www.sgpbusiness.com/company/Chiu-Teng-Construction-Co-Pte-Ltd/government-procurement/agency).

![Tender](/images/stacktheflags-2022/finding-nyan-4.png)

![Streetview](/images/stacktheflags-2022/finding-nyan-5.png)
